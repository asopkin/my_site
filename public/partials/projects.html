Randomized Network Generator

Amanda has worked on designing and creating home and enterprise network topologies with ring and star structures and OSPF/BGP routing. She developed a probabilistic enterprise environment and populated this topology with automated processes. The system was created for testing purposes and was created using a virtual machine platform.

Cozad New Ventures Contest

Amanda and her partner are working to create BABU--part mobile application and part scanning device, BABU will give users an electronic, paper-free option by eliminating the need for physical receipts. Countless paper receipts are merely thrown away, generating a staggering 1.5 million pounds of waste. Our product allows businesses and users to exchange receipt without a paper middleman. Babu will transmit information virtually, streamlining and synchronizing budgetary planning. The Babu team won Best Undergraduate Team in the 2014 Dale Cozad New Venture Competition. The team has been featured in Electronic Design magazine ECN and on the University of Illinois engineering website.
